Here is one of the nice recipes from Estonia. This is a dish for gluten - free and vegan people that is purrrrrfect for someone who wants to make a dish thats quick, and very simple. This dish also comes with a drink too.

Summer salad

Ingredients:

  • 10-20 small tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • dill
  • sundried tomatoes
  • soy sauce (optinal)

How to make:

  1. slice the cucumber into tiny bits with a knife and put into a bowl.
  2. cut the tomatoes in half and put into the bowl.
  3. sprinkle the dill into the bowl.
  4. take the sundried tomatoes put into the bowl.
  5. enjoy! (mabye with a bit of soy sauce)

Strawberry suprise

  • Lemon
  • sugar
  • 30 strawberrys
  • water

    • How to make:

    1. Pour some water into a cup.
    2. Sqeeze the lemon juice out of a lemon and put into the cup.
    3. sprinkle 20g of sugar into the cup.
    4. mash 15 of the strawberrys very well and put that into the cup.
    5. cut the rest of the strawberrys in half and put them into the cup.
