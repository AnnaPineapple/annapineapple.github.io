Summer salad

Ingredients:

  • 10-20 cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • dill
  • sundried tomatoes
  • soy sauce (optinal)

How to make:

  1. slice the cucumber into tiny bits with a knife and put into a bowl.
  2. cut the tomatoes in half and put into the bowl.
  3. cut and sprinkle the dill into the bowl.
  4. take the sundried tomatoes put into the bowl.
  5. enjoy! (mabye with a bit of soy sauce)

Strawberry suprise

Ingredients

  • Lemon
  • sugar
  • 30 - 40 strawberrys
  • water

How to make:

  1. Pour some water into a cup.
  2. Sqeeze the lemon juice out of a lemon and put into the cup.
  3. sprinkle 20g of sugar into the cup.
  4. mash 15 of the strawberrys very well and put that into the cup.
  5. cut the rest of the strawberrys in half and put them into the cup. Drink it away!
